About Me

I was born in Hong Kong in 1997, but my family immigrated to Canada when I was 3 yrs old. My parents studied at a foreign language institute in China so after coming to Canada, English was not the biggest problem. I learned English at school, while I spoke Chinese with my parents, allowing me to learn both at the same time.

I have two older sisters, one born in 1996, also in Hong Kong and the other is 1989, born in Guangzhou, China. When I was young, my entire family was here in Vancouver, but after my oldest sister graduated high school, it was only me, my other sister and my mom. My dad prefers staying in China because he is used to the culture there and has many more friends. Although I grew up in a Chinese household, my culture is more Canadian than chinese.
