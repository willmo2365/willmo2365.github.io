Four Seasons Hotel

Previous Next

Between October 2017 and May 2018, I interned at Four Seasons Hotel in Guangzhou, China. I wanted to have some real world experience as this was my first job. I wanted to be in the IT department, but they said that they do not do internships for the IT department because they don't want to switch employees all the time. Since it was not possible and I still wanted to have experience, they offered me a spot in the in room dining department. Although there were many challenges I faced, mainly language problems and talkign with guests, I still learned a lot. I managed to understand the back end of a high end hotel, something that typical guests do not know of.
